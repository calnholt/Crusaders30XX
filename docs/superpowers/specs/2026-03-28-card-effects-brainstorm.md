# Card Effects Brainstorm

Effects that modify cards and force the player to think in different ways.

## Design Constraints

- **Categories:** Restrictions + Mutations
- **Duration:** Mix of temporary and lingering
- **Source:** Primarily enemy-sourced; player-sourced only if compelling
- **Complexity:** One effect, one job; stacking creates combos naturally
- **Lingering removal:** Gameplay-driven (use the card, earn it off)
- **Organization:** By decision axis — each effect attacks a different part of the player's thought process
- **Never:** Positional/ordering effects

---

## Approved Effects

### Bound (Temporary — clears end of enemy turn)

**Type:** Restriction
**Decision axis:** "Is blocking with this card worth losing it for the rest of the fight?"

If a Bound card is used to block, it's exhausted instead of going to the discard pile. The card plays normally during Action phase — but using it defensively destroys it. This is the inverse of Recoil (which punishes NOT blocking). The player has to decide: absorb damage with something else, or sacrifice this card permanently?

### Contagious (Temporary — lasts until end of encounter)

**Type:** Restriction
**Decision axis:** "How do I get rid of this card without spreading the infection?"

When a Contagious card enters the discard pile (via discard, not via playing), the effect spreads to a random card in the discard pile. Playing the card does NOT spread it — playing is the "safe" disposal. Forces the player to actually play contagious cards rather than cycling past them, even if they're not ideal plays. Ignoring them makes the problem grow.

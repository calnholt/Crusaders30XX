# Passive Keywords

This document summarizes all passive effects in the game. Each passive has a duration type:
- **(Turn)** - Resets at the end of each turn
- **(Battle)** - Lasts for the entire battle
- **(Quest)** - Persists across battles

---

## Damage Over Time

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Burn** | At the start of your turn, take damage equal to stacks |
| **Bleed** | Lose 1 HP at the start of your turn, then remove one bleed |
| **Poison** | Every 60 seconds, lose 1 HP |
| **Frostbite** | At 3 stacks, take 3 damage and lose 3 frostbite |

---

## Defensive

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Aegis** | Prevents the next X damage from any source |
| **Armor** | Take X less damage from attacks |
| **Shield** | Prevent all damage from the first source each turn |

---

## Offensive Modifiers

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Power** | Attacks deal +X damage |
| **Aggression** | The next attack this turn gains X damage |
| **Channel** | Increases the potency of attacks |
| **Rage** | Gain X power at the start of the action phase (player) or block phase (enemy) |
| **Anathema** | When you pledge a card, the enemy loses X damage |

---

## Debuffs (Applied to Enemies)

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Stun** | Skips the next X attacks |
| **Slow** | Ambush attacks are X seconds faster |
| **Wounded** | Takes X more damage from all sources |

---

## Debuffs (Applied to Player)

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Scar** | Lose X max HP (permanent) |
| **Penance** | Attacks deal X less damage. Converts to scars at start of next battle |
| **Fear** | Attacks have X*10% chance to become ambush attacks |
| **Intimidated** | At the start of the block phase, X cards from your hand are intimidated |
| **MindFog** | At the end of your action phase, discard all cards in your hand |
| **Silenced** | Cannot play pledged cards. Remove 1 silenced at end of action phase |
| **Enflamed** | If you have 4+ courage at the end of the action phase, take X damage |

---

## Cold Mechanics

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Frozen** | Playing a frozen card: gain 1 frostbite, 50% chance to exhaust. Remove by blocking with it |
| **SubZero** | At the start of the enemy turn, freeze one card from your hand |
| **Windchill** | Whenever you block with a frozen card, gain 1 penance |
| **Frostbite** | At 3 stacks, take 3 damage and lose 3 frostbite |

---

## Enemy Passives

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Stealth** | Hides the number of attacks the monster plans |
| **Thorns** | Player gains X bleed whenever attacking this enemy |
| **Siphon** | For each courage removed from player, enemy heals X * multiplier HP |
| **Webbing** | At the start of your turn, gain X slow |
| **Inferno** | At the start of your turn, gain X burn |
| **Shackled** | At the start of block phase, shackle 2 cards. Remove by blocking with them |

---

## Utility

| Keyword | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Intellect** | Max hand size and cards drawn at start of block phase increased by X |

Player HUD UX Brainstorm

Explores cohesive layouts for temperance, HP, courage, status passives (aggression, etc.), and action points. AP is only meaningful during the player action phase; use the phase toggle to preview hide vs integrate behaviors. Target resolution: 1920x1080 (scaled in browser). Palette: black / white / red, New Rocker + Chakra Petch.

A: Current (baseline) — Matches in-game layout today. Vitals float above portrait; passives offset below HP; AP isolated near discard (bottom-left). Reads as four unrelated widgets.
  • Pros: AP corner is out of the way during enemy turns; passives keep their distinctive trapezoid look.
  • Cons: No shared frame; eye travels top-center, then down-right for passives, then far left for AP.

Cross-cutting ideas
  • Shared slant language: Temperance chunks, HP bar left edge, and passive trapezoids already share skew — unify with one panel clip-path (B, D) so they read as one kit.
  • AP without corner exile: Prefer reserved slot + opacity (B, D) or footer collapse (C) over hard hide/show at a distant anchor.
  • Courage scaling: No cap — consider shrinking font or "+N" overflow past 2 digits in circle badge.
  • Passive density: Keep trapezoids for 1-3 effects; switch to mini-pips (F) or outlined chips when count is high.
  • Enemy phase: Same frame, dim non-relevant segments; avoids layout shift when AP appears.
